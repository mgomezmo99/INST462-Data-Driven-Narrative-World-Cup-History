Teams

Group stage standings — select a year, filter by group, sort by any column

In this section, you will be able to view the teams that made it to the World Cup in participation being in the Group Stage per World Cup year. You can select the year, Group letter and sort the teams in Alphabetical order, group, points from lowest to highest, as well as wins, draws, lost, goal in favor (GF), goals against (GA), and goal difference (GD).

Head-to-Head

Look up every match played between any two nations across all 20 World Cups

In Head-To-Head, you will be able to look for a team country and their results. If they faced each other the display will appear but if there is no history, the data will not display. Once you have selected the teams you will see their wins, draws, and goals scored. At the bottom there will be the year they competed and scored based on Group Stage, Round of 16, Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, and Final. Give it a try!
